Relationship Viewer

Introduction

This is a simple tool to visualize the relationships between dataflows. It is intended to be used for staging and testing purposes.

Usage

In the top navigation bar, you will find three sections:

  • Singles: This section shows all the dataflows that have no dependencies and are not dependencies of any other dataflow.

  • Parents With Parents: This section shows all the dataflows that have dependencies and are also dependencies of other dataflows.

  • Parents Without Parents: This section shows all the dataflows that have dependencies but are not dependencies of any other dataflow.
